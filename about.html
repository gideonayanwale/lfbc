HISTORY:

To the glory of God, the ministry held the first meeting on Friday 17th May, 1991 in a classroom at Ijeru Baptist Day School, Caretaker area,Ogbomoso.The first organised worship service took place on Sunday 19th May, 1991 at the same venue. The attendance that day was twenty(26) consisting of both the children and adults. The venue is the same for three years (1991-1994) before God provided the present auditorium at Ologbon-lo-nisimi area,Ogbomoso. Initially, the church was bearing Love Fellowship Bible Church until 2010 when the church was registered asLove Fellowship Bible Christian International. The church was establish on 17th May,1991 with the following founders and their wives:

  • Rev'd & Past.(Sis.) Victor Tunde Bose Dare -The General Overseer,
  • Past.& Mrs. Olubilola T. Oladipupo ,
  • Past. and Sis. Oyerinde ,
  • Past. Z.O. Olawuyi ,
  • Past. & sis. S.O Oyekale ,
  • Evan. and Sis S.A. Areo

The church teaches the bible through conference such as women of love conference, men of love conference, youth of love conference and december retreat which as started since 1995.

The ministry has acquired a campground at Aipo in Oriire local Govt. Area of Ogbomoso, Oyo state, Nigeria in 2009 and December retreat was held at Garden Of Love for the first time in 2015. The church present God's word in drama and the ministry has produced some titled:

  1. Idajo Alagbara Nla,
  2. Okoto Idile (part 1-3),
  3. Gboongbo Ikoro,
  4. Amure Ogo ,
  5. Fitla Ogo,
  6. Agbelebu ogo, and
  7. Abela Eledumare which will be released soon.

To God be the glory, LFBC has established a theology called LOVE FELLOWSHIP THEOLOGY on Friday 8th April,2016 at Garden Of Love, Aipo with three lecturers:Rev'd Dr.Victor Tunde Dare, Pastor Dr. S.T. Akanbi and Pastor Dr. (Sis.) Bose Ola. Dare.

New Pastors were elected for training and church management on Sunday 7th February, 2016 and they are twelve in number: eleven male and a female.

ABOUT THE GENERAL OVERSEER:

Rev'd Dr. Dare Victor Tunde is the general overseer of love fellowship christian ministries.He obtained Masters of Biblical studies And Doctor of ministries in Church Administrations from ECWA Theological Seminary,Igbaja,Kwara State,Nigeria.He is a teacher,a pastor,a drama ministerand a discipler. He teaches through expository and dramatic illustration of GOD's word. His ardent love for making GOD's word known makes him organise conferences and retreats for various grops of people (youth, men and women among others) and produce christian films.

Rev'd Dr. Dare is an epitome of devotion, dedication, commitment and perseverance. He is a man with a single focus, who is always found in his Father's business. He currently lives in Ogbomoso area of Oyo state, Nigeria.
He is happily married and blessed with children. He can be contacted by visiting his house,church or by calling: 08035700092.
